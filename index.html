Q:2.Query the collection ""employee"" and list all the documents

Answer 2. db.employee.find({})

Q:3.Query the collection ""employee"" and list the employees who are having salary more than 30000

Answer: 3. db.employee.find({"salary":{$gt:"30000"}})


Q:4. Query the collection ""employee"" and list the employees who are having experience more than 2 years.

Answer:4. db.employee.find({"experience":{$gt:"2"}})


Q:5. Query the collection ""employee"" and list the employees who are graduated after 2015 and having experience more than 1 year

Answer:5. db.employee.find({"yearGrad":{$gt:"2015"},"overallExp":{$gt:"1"}})


Q:6. Query the collection ""employee"" and update the salary of the employee whose salary is greater than 70000 to 65000.

Answer.6. db.employee.updateMany({salary:{$gt:"70000"}},{$set:{salary:"65000"}})


Q:7. Delete all the documents from ""employee"" where last company is Y"

Answer:7. db.employee.deleteMany({lastCompany:"Y"})
